Lunch Recipe

Chicken Salad

Step1:Gather all ingredients.
Step2:Place almonds in a frying pan. Toast over medium-high heat, shaking frequently. Watch carefully, as they burn easily.
Step3:Mix together mayonnaise, lemon juice, and pepper in a medium bowl.
Step4:Toss with chicken, toasted almonds, and celery.
Step5:Enjoy!

Dinner Recipe

Okonomiyaki

Step1:Whisk together the okonomiyaki flour (or flour + baking powder + soy sauce/dashi powder) and water.
Step2:Finely chop the cabbage and spring onion. Add the chopped vegetables to the batter.
Step3:Heat the oil in a large frying pan or griddle, on medium heat.
Step4:Add the slices of protein on top of the okonomiyaki and cover with a lid. Cook for about 5 minutes covered.
Step5:After the bottom of the pancake is browned, flip it over and cook covered for another 5 minutes.
